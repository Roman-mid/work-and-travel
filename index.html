History Center

The construction of Big Ben began on September 28, 1843. But already on May 21, 1859, the clock was put into action for the first time. Until today, they are considered one of the largest in the world. The creation of dials is a jeweler's work. They are made of fragile opal glass, additionally framed with gilded frames. The clock is inscribed in Latin, which translates as "God save our Queen Victoria." Big Ben performs a global function - it is on it that the countdown of the new year begins. Information about its beginning comes when the clock hand first strikes January 1.
